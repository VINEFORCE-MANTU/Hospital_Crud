MediTrack HMS is a modern Hospital Management System developed using ASP.NET Boilerplate (ABP) and Angular. The system is designed to streamline hospital operations, improve healthcare management, and provide intelligent assistance through Artificial Intelligence.

What is MediTrack HMS?

MediTrack HMS is an enterprise-level healthcare management platform that helps hospitals and clinics efficiently manage doctors, patients, admissions, rooms, beds, and healthcare operations.

The system simplifies day-to-day administrative activities such as patient registration, doctor management, patient admissions, room allocation, and bed occupancy tracking while ensuring data consistency and operational efficiency.

Core Modules

  • Doctor Management – Manage doctor profiles, qualifications, specialization, and availability.
  • Patient Management – Register and maintain patient records, personal information, and medical details.
  • Patient Admission Management – Handle patient admissions, diagnosis, doctor assignment, approval workflows, and discharge processes.
  • Room Management – Manage room categories including General, Private, ICU, and Special Rooms.
  • Bed Management – Track bed availability, occupancy status, and room-wise bed allocation.
  • Doctor AI Assistant – AI-powered medical assistant that helps doctors discuss symptoms, diseases, treatment approaches, differential diagnoses, medications, and healthcare guidelines using advanced Large Language Models (LLMs).

AI-Powered Doctor Assistant

MediTrack HMS includes an integrated Doctor AI Assistant which provides intelligent medical discussions and healthcare support for authorized doctors.

The AI Assistant can help healthcare professionals:

  • Discuss symptoms and diseases.
  • Provide medical information and guidance.
  • Suggest possible differential diagnoses.
  • Explain treatment approaches.
  • Support clinical decision discussions.
  • Provide healthcare knowledge assistance.

The AI Assistant is available through a floating chatbot interface and can be accessed from anywhere in the application by authorized doctors.

System Architecture

MediTrack HMS follows a clean N-Layer Architecture to ensure scalability, maintainability, and separation of concerns.

  • Domain Layer – Business entities and domain logic.
  • Application Layer – Application services, DTOs, validation, and business workflows.
  • Infrastructure Layer – Repository pattern, Entity Framework Core, and database operations.
  • Presentation Layer – Angular-based responsive user interface.

The project also follows Domain Driven Design (DDD) principles to improve maintainability, flexibility, and code organization.

Technologies Used
